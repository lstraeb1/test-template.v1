Our Story

Established in 2022, SUB-G is a dedicated martial arts school that emphasizes a deep understanding of Grappling Martial Arts. Our approach blends foundational techniques that have shaped grappling over the years with contemporary methods and practical scenarios.

Tyler Roberts, who founded SUB-G BJJ, also leads as the head instructor at our facility in Port Jervis, NY. With a profound commitment to Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, Tyler focuses on creating a nurturing and inclusive atmosphere, welcoming students from all walks of life to explore and grow in the art.
