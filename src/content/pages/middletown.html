Middletown, NY Facility

Train in out Middletown facility with spacious mats, premium amenities, and access to the full Positive Fitness gym experience.

Try Out SUB-G's Brand New Middletown Facility

As of October 2024 SUB-G Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu has expanded to a new, state-of-the-art facility in Middletown, NY. The spacious training area provides plenty of room for classes, open mat sessions, and specialized training programs. In addition, members have exclusive access to Positive Fitness, a fully-equipped gym that includes showers, locker rooms, and high-quality workout equipment. This new facility is designed to enhance your training experience, offering everything you need for a comprehensive fitness routine under one roof. Whether you're here for BJJ or a full-body workout, SUB-G's new Middletown location has you covered.

Showers, Locker-Rooms, & Weight Training Equipment

Located inside Positive Fitness Training at 130 Dolson Ave, the Middletown SUB-G Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu facility offers convenient access to showers, locker rooms, and top-tier weight training equipment. After your BJJ session, you can refresh in the clean, spacious locker rooms and take advantage of the full gym setup. Positive Fitness is fully equipped with free weights, machines, and cardio gear, providing everything you need to supplement your training and keep yourself in peak physical condition.

  • Showers and locker-rooms included for your convenience
  • Large mat space for BJJ and martial arts training
  • Access to Positive Fitness Training's weight and cardio equipment
