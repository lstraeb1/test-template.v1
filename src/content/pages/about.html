Who We Are

Grappling In Orange County, NY Since 2022

At Sub-G, we're more than just a Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu academy; we're a family united by a passion for martial arts and a commitment to personal growth. Nestled within both Port Jervis and Middletown, we provide a welcoming environment where practitioners of all levels can thrive.

Whether you're a seasoned competitor or stepping onto the mat for the first time, our experienced instructors are dedicated to guiding you on your journey to mastering the art of Jiu-Jitsu and Martial Arts. Join us in the art of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu at Sub-G, where every roll is an opportunity for self-discovery and growth.

Coach Tyler

Your Black Belt Coach For All Things Martial Arts

Coach Tyler Roberts, a skilled Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu black belt, is dedicated to fostering growth in students of all ages. His passion for the art of BJJ is matched by his commitment to teaching and mentoring students at every level. Whether working with beginners or advanced practitioners, Coach Roberts tailors his instruction to meet each student's needs, ensuring they develop the confidence, discipline, and technique required for success both on and off the mat.

Coach Tyler believes that BJJ is not just a physical activity but a transformative tool that builds mental toughness and self-discipline. He creates a supportive learning environment where students feel encouraged to push beyond their limits and achieve their goals. His ability to connect with younger students and adults alike has made him a trusted mentor, guiding his students through the challenges of BJJ while instilling lifelong values of perseverance and respect.
